The telephone was created during the Industrial Revolution in 1876.


The telephone was created to help improve the communication problem that most people experience. The problem was they couldn't send messages quick enough or they had to use morse code just to send a simple message.


The first patent was made by Alexender Graham Bell which could replicate a human voice at the other end.
Telephones are known as duplex devices, which meant they can send and recieve data on a single or multiple lines at once. Alexender was a Scottish Inventor which created multiple other things which would help the human race in the future.
